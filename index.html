About Us

Your Home in the Mountains

Nestled in the pristine village of Bajwadpul near Saraikhet, Almora, Vaibhav Home Stay offers an authentic Kumaoni hospitality experience surrounded by the majestic Himalayan ranges.

The climate here ranges from 1°C to 27°C, with occasional snowfall in peak winters. We are approximately 90 km from Jim Corbett National Park — about 3 hours by road — making us the perfect base for wildlife enthusiasts and nature lovers alike.
